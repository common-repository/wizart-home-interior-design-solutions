== Description ==

[Wizart AR Room Visualizer](https://www.wizart.ai/) - **the unmatched and realistic home improvement product visualization tool** driving customer engagement and boosting sales. Wizart offers a set of cutting-edge solutions for your home improvement online catalogs, web stores, and eCommerce businesses.

Wizart AR Room Visualizer is based on a neural network that means continuous visualization quality improvement. Visualizer has a user-friendly interface and your customers get the visualization result in three simple steps: they upload their interior, pick the product, and see if it fits their interior. As a result, retailers and manufacturers **build customer loyalty and increase customer engagement up to 27%.**

**Wizart AR Room Visualizer works with any type of products:**
- Wallcoverings
- Murals
- Flooring
- Paints
- Tiles
- Wall art
- Carpets & rugs
- Custom-made home décor products
- Any other products upon your request

Wizart AR Room Visualizer **WordPress Plugin** allows you to install the Visualizer in a few clicks. Please note that the Visualizer usage is based on subscription payments. See our detailed pricing [here](https://wizart.ai/pricing).

== Installation ==

1. Upload Wizart AR Room Visualizer WordPress Plugin to your WordPress admin panel from the Plugins tab.
2. Follow our step-by-step [guide](https://wizart.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/WDP/pages/1159364737/Integration+instructions+WordPress+Plugin) for a super-easy integration.
